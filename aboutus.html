Bringing colors to life since 1998 with premium quality paints and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Mr. Rajiv Sharma, ColorCraft Paints began as a small retail shop in Mumbai with a vision to provide high-quality paints that would transform spaces and lives. What started as a modest venture has now grown into one of India's most trusted paint brands.

Our journey has been colored with challenges and triumphs, but our commitment to quality has never wavered. We've expanded from a single store to over 200 outlets across India, serving millions of customers who trust us to bring their visions to life.

At ColorCraft, we believe that colors have the power to influence emotions, enhance productivity, and create environments that nurture well-being. This philosophy drives our innovation and fuels our passion for creating products that exceed expectations.

Today, we offer a comprehensive range of interior and exterior paints, primers, and specialty finishes that cater to diverse needs and preferences. Our eco-friendly formulations reflect our commitment to sustainability and responsible business practices.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Pune, Chennai, and Delhi NCR are equipped with cutting-edge technology and adhere to the highest quality and safety standards. Each facility is ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified, reflecting our commitment to quality management and environmental responsibility.

We employ rigorous testing at every stage of production to ensure consistency, durability, and performance. Our advanced color-matching technology enables us to offer over 2,000 shades with precision and accuracy.

Our R&D team continuously works on developing innovative formulations that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional, durable, and eco-friendly. We invest significantly in research to stay ahead of industry trends and environmental regulations.

With a combined production capacity of over 100 million liters annually, we can meet the growing demand for our products while maintaining the quality standards that have made ColorCraft a trusted name in the Indian paint industry.
